# FAQ

## Q: Can I add more than 4 teams?

> A: Yes! The system is designed to be extensible. You can add as many teams as you want through the initialization API.

## Q: How accurate are the predictions?

> A: The Monte Carlo simulation with 10,000 iterations provides statistical accuracy similar to professional sports analytics systems. Accuracy increases as more matches are played.

## Q: Can I modify team strengths?

> A: Yes! You can send custom team objects with any power rating (1-100) when initializing the league.

## Q: Is the match simulation truly random?

> A: The simulation uses controlled randomness based on statistical distributions, similar to real football matches. Strong teams are more likely to win, but upsets can happen.

## Q: Can I export the results?

> A: The current version doesn't include export functionality, but you can easily extend the API to add CSV or JSON export features.
